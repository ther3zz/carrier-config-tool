Transfer DIDs

Transfer DIDs Between Subaccounts

Move phone numbers from one subaccount to another under the same primary account. This is a permanent and irreversible operation.

Request rate/concurrency can be adjusted via the ⚙ settings icon in the page header.

Primary Account Credentials

The primary account is used to authenticate the transfer. Both source and destination must be subaccounts under this primary.

Subaccount Selection

First, fetch the list of subaccounts. Then select the source (from) and destination (to) accounts below.
